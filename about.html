Our Beginning

A Dream Born in Kabankalan

Luzville Café started with one dream — to create a peaceful place where people can enjoy meaningful moments together.

From morning coffee to late-night conversations, we want every customer to feel welcomed, valued, and right at home. Since opening in 2024, we've been honored to be part of your daily routine.
